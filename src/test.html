Wyoming Summer

3 parts

TGR Optics athlete Amy David caught the bug to track down snow last summer during the peak of sweltering temps. Ideas started brewing and soon enough, a Wyoming getaway was born. With Tami Razzinger and Veronica Paulson on board, this lady crew mapped out a three-part adventure starting from Jackson Hole Mountain Resort’s iconic tram and ending in beautiful Pinedale, Wyoming, the gateway to the Wind River Range. Talk about a good ol’ mountain adventure. Follow-up for trip planning ideas of your own.
